Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application "Whose Region?" ("the App") handles information.

1. Overview

The App is a reference tool that helps users identify vehicle region codes for Russia, Belarus, and Kazakhstan. The App itself does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or address.

2. Information Collected

The App itself does not directly collect or store personal data entered by users for identification purposes.

However, the App uses third-party advertising services, including Yandex Advertising, which may collect certain technical and device-related information automatically in accordance with their own privacy practices.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Yandex Ads. These advertising services may collect and process information such as:

  • device identifiers;
  • IP address;
  • app interaction data;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • diagnostic and performance data.

This information may be used to provide, personalize, and measure ads, prevent fraud, and improve advertising services.

Please review Yandex’s privacy documentation for more information about how Yandex handles data.

4. Use of Information

Any information processed by third-party advertising providers may be used for:

  • displaying ads in the App;
  • measuring ad performance;
  • maintaining service security and fraud prevention;
  • improving ad relevance and service quality.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by third-party service providers such as Yandex Ads as necessary to provide advertising functionality.

6. Data Retention

The App itself does not maintain a user account database or store personal profiles. Any retention of technical or advertising-related data is governed by the policies of the third-party providers used in the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review the issue.

8. Your Choices

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to limit ad personalization by adjusting your device privacy or advertising settings.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may open third-party apps or services such as Google Maps or Yandex Maps. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
