Privacy Policy for “Schulte Squares”

Effective date: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed by the “Schulte Squares” mobile application (the “App”), for what purposes, and how.

1. General Information

The App is designed to train attention and visual search speed (Schulte tables) and operates locally on the user’s device.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

During normal use, the App may store local technical data on your device, such as game settings and local records/progress.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • full name, email address, or phone number;
  • location data;
  • contacts;
  • photos/videos/files;
  • payment information;
  • advertising identifiers for profiling.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not share user data with third parties.

5. Internet and Servers

The App does not require account registration and does not use developer-operated servers to store user data. All game data and settings are stored locally on the device.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is stored locally until the user clears app data via system settings or uninstalls the App.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure proper local data handling within platform capabilities. Device access control remains the user’s responsibility.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality or legal requirements change. The current version will be published with an updated effective date.
